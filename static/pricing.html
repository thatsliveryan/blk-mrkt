FAQ

When does the new fee rate kick in?

Immediately upon successful payment. Any drop sale processed after your subscription activates uses your new tier's fee rate. There's no waiting period.

What happens if I cancel?

Your tier stays active until the end of the billing period. After that, you automatically revert to the Free tier (15% fee). Your drops, fans, and data are never affected.

Does the Stripe fee change across tiers?

No. Stripe charges 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction regardless of your plan — that's Stripe's standard rate and goes directly to them. The percentages listed in our tier cards are BLK MRKT's platform fee only.

Can I upgrade mid-month?

Yes. The new fee rate applies immediately to all future transactions. You'll be billed the prorated difference on your next invoice.

What's the monthly boost budget?

Each tier includes a monthly cap on how much you can spend on Spark / Fire / Inferno boosts. The cap resets on the 1st of each month. Label tier has no cap.

Is there a free trial?

The Free tier is free forever with unlimited drops. No credit card required. We don't offer trials on paid tiers — the math speaks for itself once you're making sales.

Do fans need to pay anything?

Fans never pay a subscription. They pay the price the artist sets on each drop (or nothing for free drops). The platform fee is only charged when a paid drop is claimed.
